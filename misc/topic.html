Pros and Cons of ChatGPT

ChatGPT is a chatbot model released by OpenAI, that generated widespread attention and discussion globally.

It is a language model based on the GPT- 3.5 architecture and holds promise of bringing about unprecedented digital experiences and enriching peoples live.

However there are also its risks which has caused the chatbot to be banned or have set limitations in multiple schools across the globe. This is due to the how effective ChatGPT is which has caused many to discuss and worry about how this would affect our mind
