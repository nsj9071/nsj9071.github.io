CTiS: The Pros and Cons of ChatGPT

ChatGPT is a chatbot model released by OpenAI, that generated widespread attention and discussion globally. It is a language model based on the GPT- 3.5 architecture and holds promise of bringing about unprecedented digital experiences and enriching peoples live. However there are also its risks which has caused the chatbot to be banned in multple schools across the globe.
