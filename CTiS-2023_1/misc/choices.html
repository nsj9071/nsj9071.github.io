Technology Choices

ChatGPT is very useful in terms of learning and other academic purposes. However, if we rely on this platform too much to operate on tasks that even our minds can perform, this is where the disadvantages take over. It will harm the mental state of the mind that is responsible for creative and critical thinking. So although we find ChatGPT helpful it needs limitations to ensure we can still do work without it.
